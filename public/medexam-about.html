Our Story

Why We Built This

Preparing for the UCAT is stressful enough without having to spend hundreds of pounds on preparation materials. We saw students paying £200+ for courses that were outdated, confusing, and didn't reflect the real exam.

We set out to create a platform that gives every aspiring doctor — regardless of background — access to genuinely high-quality UCAT preparation at a price that's fair. £29.99 for a full year. That's it.
